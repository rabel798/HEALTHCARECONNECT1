Meet Dr. Richa Sharma

Vision that shines, care that matters

Dr. Richa Sharma is a practicing Ophthalmologist with over 15 years of experience. After finishing her Master of Surgery from prestigious Aligarh Muslim University, Dr. Richa finished her Fellowship in pediatric ophthalmology and squint from Prestigious Aravind Eye Hospital, Coimbatore.

Later, she finished her Fellowship in advanced pediatric ophthalmology from Boston Children's Hospital, Harvard Medical School. Dr. Richa has many international and national research papers in her name.

Her gentle approach and commitment to excellence ensure that each patient receives personalized attention and the highest standard of care. Dr. Richa's clinic serves as a hub for your family and kid's eyes, offering comprehensive services for patients of all ages.

Professional Excellence:
  • Over 15 years of experience in Ophthalmology
  • Master of Surgery from Aligarh Muslim University
  • Fellowship in Pediatric Ophthalmology & Squint - Aravind Eye Hospital, Coimbatore
  • Fellowship in Advanced Pediatric Ophthalmology - Boston Children's Hospital, Harvard Medical School
  • Multiple international and national research publications
  • Specializes in Adult and Pediatric Eye Care
