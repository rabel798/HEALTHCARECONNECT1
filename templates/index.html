Meet Dr. Richa Sharma

Vision that shines, care that matters

Dr. Richa Sharma is a highly qualified ophthalmologist dedicated to providing exceptional eye care services. With her extensive training and experience, she brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every patient interaction.

Her gentle approach and commitment to excellence ensure that each patient receives personalized attention and the highest standard of care. Dr. Richa's clinic serves as a hub for your family and kid's eyes, offering comprehensive services for patients of all ages.

Qualifications:
  • MBBS, MS, FPOS
  • Fellowship in Aravind Eye Hospital, Coimbatore
  • Fellowship from Boston Children's Hospital, Harvard Medical School
  • Specializes in Adult and Pediatric Eye Care
